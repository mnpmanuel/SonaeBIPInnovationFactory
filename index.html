ABOUT this program

Innovation PowerUP is an internal innovation program promoted by Sonae Sierra’s Innovation Office, to support and accelerate the implementation of business innovative projects, coming from any Sierra's employee or team.

The goal is to select projects that are either waiting for resources to be implemented, or projects that are already being implemented but need to be accelerated, and provide them with an opportunity to get additional support from the Senior Executives and the Innovation Office.

Innovation PowerUP is one of the 3 programs founded and managed by the Innovation Office, with the focus of fostering an innovative culture within Sierra.

Sierra Tank - Based on an ignite type-of pitching, this program is aimed at fetching and accelerating new ideas coming from any employee. The rewarding is then based on a Tank format where Sierra's Senior Executives are invited to bid on each pitch, enabling them with some necesary resourcing towards a future implementation.

Innovation Awards - Focused on the recognition of projects already implemented, excelling on the delivery of its innovative factor. The rewarding is targeted to the team behind each initiative.

Innovation PowerUP, is therefore, supposed to be the innovation program in between, since, it targets projects that were already discussed or, are in the process of being implemented, with the objective of accelerating the whole process.

Innovation PowerUP - If you have an innovative project that needs more speed, energy and fuel… this is the right program and the right moment.

HOW IT WORKS

Sonae Sierra Innovation PowerUP will happen from June to October 2019 with several phases and milestones, ending up in a final pitch session for Sierra's Senior Executives.
In order to participate, all applicants, individually or in a team format, should go through the following steps:

  • From June 1 to 30

    APPLICATION PERIOD

    We know that everybody sometimes procrastinates and leave things until the last moment… it’s only human. However, studies demonstrate that the best applications are not the ones submitted at 23:59 of the last day. Don’t over-think it and apply NOW.

  • June 19 @ 15:00 GMT

    ASK-ME-ANYTHING WEBCAST

    If you’re still thinking about applying or you have doubts, this session is for you. The objective of this session is that you ask us those hard questions that you might have.

  • From July 1 to 15

    SELECTION PHASE

    The Innovation Office along with the Innovation Committee, will evaluate every application according to the criteria defined by the regulation in order to select up to 10 applications that will be the finalists.

  • July 16

    FINALISTS ANNOUNCEMENT

    On this beautiful day, we will announce up to 10 finalists that will have the opportunity of participating in the next phase of Innovation PowerUP. This shortlist will be announced by e-mail, and most probably the Innovation Office will ask some homework to kickstart the engines.

  • September 4

    WORKSHOP WEBINAR

    In order to start building an impactful presentation and a more solid business-case, this remote workshop will allow you to have a flash-training on how to do it better.

  • From September 5 to 23

    VIRTUAL MENTORING

    Because each team and project are unique in their own ways, our team will provide every single one of the finalists with an individual virtual mentoring session.

  • September 23

    PROPOSAL RE-SUBMISSION

    With the virtual training and mentoring, the projects should be improved, converted in a proposal presentation and re-submitted for a final selection.

  • From September 24 to 30

    FINAL SELECTION FOR PITCH

    The innovation committee will select up to 5 finalists for the final phase of the program.

  • October 15 @ PORTO, Portugal

    PITCH BOOTCAMP

    In order to prepare the project pitch, every team will have the opportunity to be part of a 1 day training session to improve and rehearse the final presentation.

  • October 16 @ PORTO, Portugal

    FINAL PITCH

    Finally, a selection of up to 5 projects will pitch to the Senior executives for a chance of getting additional support to accelerate their innovation projects.

  • October 31

    WINNERS ANNOUNCEMENT

    In this day the winners will be announced and most probably drinks will be shared among the teams, finalists and of course the Innovation Office.
